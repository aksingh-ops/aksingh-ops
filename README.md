## 📂 Featured Projects

### ✈️ AA Crew Absence Prediction & Reserve Optimization
> End-to-end ML system predicting American Airlines crew sick-calls and optimizing reserve staffing

**Stack:** Python · XGBoost · BTS Flight Data · SHAP

- Built on **820,876 real BTS flight records** with XGBoost achieving **AUC 0.87, Recall 0.91**
- Developed original **Schedule Stress Score** feature — ranked **#4 of 35 features** by importance
- Optimized reserve staffing to reduce over-reserve costs while maintaining operational coverage

[→ View Repository](https://github.com/aksingh-ops/AA-Crew-Absence-Prediction-Reserve-Optimization)

---

### 👥 Employee Attrition Risk Scoring
> Individual-level 90-day voluntary attrition prediction with SHAP explainability

**Stack:** Python · Random Forest · IBM HR Dataset · SHAP

- Predicted 90-day voluntary attrition risk on **1,470 IBM HR records** — AUC 0.748, Recall 0.511
- Implemented **SHAP explainability** to surface top risk drivers per individual employee
- Designed for actionable HR intervention, not just classification

[→ View Repository](https://github.com/aksingh-ops/employee-attrition-risk-scoring)
